Peaceful Escapes at Kuzhupilly

Reconnect with yourself in the serene lap of nature and conservation.

Back to Kuzhupilly Resort

Zen Yoga & Meditation

Begin your day with inner peace. Our sunrise yoga and meditation sessions take place on our dedicated wooden deck, surrounded by lush flora and the gentle sounds of the sea. Led by experienced practitioners, these sessions are designed to harmonize your body and spirit. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced yogi, the serenity of Kuzhupilly provides the perfect environment for practice.

  • Timings: Daily at 6:30 AM
  • Location: Outdoor Zen Deck
  • Inclusions: Yoga mats & Herbal tea

Casual Beach Volleyball

For those who enjoy active recreation, our dedicated sand court awaits. Engage in friendly matches with other guests or organize family tournaments in the soft golden sands. It's a great way to stay active, meet fellow travelers, and enjoy the refreshing sea breeze. We provide professional-grade equipment and can help organize matches on request.

  • Availability: Open all day
  • Equipment: Provided by resort
  • Setup: Regulation-size sand court

Marine Turtle Conservation

Become part of a vital ecosystem effort. Kuzhupilly is a seasonal nesting ground for local sea turtles. We collaborate with local conservationists to protect these nesting sites. Guests can participate in educational sessions and, during the season, witness the magical release of hatchlings into the sea. It's a humbling and educational experience for all ages.

  • Focus: Education & Protection
  • Seasonality: Generally November to March
  • Inclusions: Expert-led briefing

Celestial Star Gazing

Experience the cosmos from one of the darkest beachfronts in the region. Without the light pollution of the city, the night sky at Kuzhupilly is breathtaking. We provide high-powered telescopes and guided constellations tours. Learn about astronomical phenomena and traditional maritime navigation techniques used by Kerala's sailors for centuries.

  • Availability: Clear nights only
  • Equipment: High-powered telescope
  • Inclusions: Guided celestial tour
