About Azure Bay

Nestled in Nature. Crafted in Luxury.

Azure Bay Resorts is a premium hospitality brand offering immersive nature stays across Kerala’s most scenic destinations — Munnar, Wayanad, Cherai, and Kuzhupilly.

Designed to blend luxury with the serenity of nature, each Azure Bay property delivers curated experiences in hills, forests, beaches, and backwaters. From the mist-covered tea gardens of Munnar to the golden sands of Cherai, our resorts are sanctuaries of peace and elegance.

Our mission is to provide more than just a stay; we offer a connection to the pristine soul of Kerala. Every detail of our service is crafted to ensure that your experience is as unique as the landscape it inhabits.
