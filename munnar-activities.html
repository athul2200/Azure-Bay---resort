Adventures at Birds Valley

Immerse yourself in the tranquility and thrill of the Munnar highlands.

Back to Munnar Resort

Guided Nature Walks

Embark on an enchanting journey through our 6-acre sanctuary and the surrounding emerald hills. Our expert naturalists will lead you through hidden trails, pointing out rare flora and the vibrant birdlife that defines the region. These walks are designed for all age groups and offer a perfect blend of exercise and environmental education.

  • Duration: 2-3 Hours
  • Best Time: Early Morning (6:30 AM)
  • Inclusions: Guide & Refreshments

Plantation Tea Tours

Discover the journey of your morning brew. Take a guided tour through Munnar's world-famous tea estates. Learn the delicate process of picking the perfect leaves and witness the traditional methods used in factory processing. The tour culminates in a professional tea-tasting session where you can savor the different grades of high-altitude tea.

  • Duration: Half Day
  • Highlights: Factory Visit & Tasting
  • Transport: Provided by Resort

Starlit Campfires

As evening mists roll in, gather around our signature campfire pits located at scenic vantage points. Share stories, enjoy traditional snacks, and witness the mountain sky clear into a canopy of stars. It's the perfect way to unwind after a day of adventure, offering a warm and communal atmosphere for families and couples alike.

  • Frequency: Every Evening (Weather permitting)
  • Setup: Traditional stone hearths
  • Inclusions: Music & BBQ on request

Avian Photography Tours

Designed for enthusiasts and professional photographers, our bird-watching tours leverage the resort's unique position as a natural bird haven. With over 60 indigenous species spotted on-site, including the Nilgiri Flycatcher and Malabar Whistling Thrush, our guides will help you set up at the best vantage points for that perfect shot.

  • Equipment: Binoculars provided
  • Specialty: Rare endemic species
  • Best Season: October to March
